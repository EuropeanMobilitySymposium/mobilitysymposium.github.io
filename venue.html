The Sky Deck, University of Exeter

23–24 September 2026. Sessions take place in the Sky Deck on the University of Exeter campus.

Getting here

Sessions take place in the Sky Deck, on the University of Exeter campus, within easy reach of Exeter city centre and with strong rail, road and air connections to the rest of the UK and Europe.

  • Venue The Sky Deck, University of Exeter
  • Address University of Exeter, Stocker Road, Exeter, EX4 4PY, UK
  • By rail Exeter St Davids and Exeter Central stations, direct from London in ~2 hours
  • By air Exeter Airport; or Bristol / Heathrow with onward rail
  • By road Close to the M5; on-campus and city parking available
